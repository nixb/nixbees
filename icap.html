Industry competence amplification program (ICAP)


We are pleased to propose an Industry competence amplification program in your esteemed institution to integrate the engineering education with the latest cutting edge technology such as IoT, Blockchain and 3D Printer. This initiative is an Industry competence amplification program (ICAP), will be a joint collaboration between the Host College and nixbees where the latter intends to provide boost for research, innovation and technical skill building through upgraded practical industry oriented curriculum to the students of your esteemed institution. This initiative will serve as bridge to connect the industry and academia and create a stronger collaboration between the two stakeholders. This is envisioned to be a one year program which will provide nixbees to impart the necessary skill set to the students and get them more credible in the way the industry/corporate imagines, thereby increasing the chances of smart employability and Entrepreneurship.

Student ICAP registration link http://bit.do/nixicap

Nixbees will provide in-house support to train, operate and manage the resources needed for smooth sailing of this initiative. More details can be found in the attached document regarding the needs of the initiative. We hope to bring out the credibility to your esteemed institution by this joint collaboration and make the students better informed about the technology and career.

Benefits to the Institute

  1. Build industrial relations
  2. Makes the placement process easier
  3. Improve institutional credibility & branding
  4. Helps in retention of the students
  5. Improvement in teaching learning process
  1. Curriculum revision can be made based on feedback from Industry/ students
  2. Long term provides in-depth and freedom to continuously evaluate the short comings
  3. No need of investing on proprietary software or hardware resources which hampers the reuse modularity and burdens the pocket
