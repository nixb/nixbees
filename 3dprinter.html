Usage of 3D Printer


Aerospace

All for the purpose of making interstellar exploration more habitable.
Architecture

3D printers help cut down manpower and time when it comes to visualizing designs for clients.
Auotomative

Companies are using it to produce not just parts, but tools, jigs and fixtures.
Comercial products

With 3D printing,Commercial products manufacturers like need the flexibility to make great products in small, medium or large volumes.
Consumer Products

3D Printing technology helps manufacturers meet the growing need for small series of personalized parts.
Defence

3D printing gives government, military and defense manufacturers the freedom to design a single end-use part, quickly create low-volume tooling, or build complex, precise prototypes.

Education

Entertainment

3D Printing helps the producers, filmmakers or even prop makers on different levels, to save time and money.
